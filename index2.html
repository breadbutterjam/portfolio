About Me

I love to create intuitive, engaging user experiences that make people's lives easier. With over 12+ years of experience, I've designed user experiences for projects of various sizes—from small interactives to complex learning management systems and enterprise applications.

I started off as a developer and then ventured into UX design on an adventure to find something challenging. This unique combination allows me to bridge the gap between design vision and technical implementation.

I specialize in problem framing, requirement analysis, and simplifying complex systems. My experience spans building and leading UX teams, delivering projects end-to-end in cross-functional environments, and aligning user needs with technical feasibility and business goals.

When I'm not at my desk creating great products, you can find me making soulful images, going on long drives, or savoring the perfect cup of coffee.
